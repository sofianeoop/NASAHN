Hardware Engineering

Hardware engineering is a discipline that combines principles of computer science, electrical engineering, and project management to design, develop, test, and maintain hardware systems. It is a systematic approach to creating hardware that meets specific requirements, is reliable, and can be maintained over time.

Certificats

Certified Embedded Systems Engineer (CESE)

Description: This certification demonstrates skills in developing IoT systems on Microsoft Azure.

Description: This certificate validates expertise in designing and building embedded systems on Google Cloud.
